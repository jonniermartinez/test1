# Gurwi: The Shipathon Winner

Gurwi is an innovative AI startup that recently made waves by winning the prestigious Shipathon, securing a $15,000 prize. This victory stands as a testament to the team's dedication and the groundbreaking nature of their AI technology.

## The Victory

On October 14, 2025, Gurwi announced their triumph: "We Won the Shipathon, We Got $15,000 - Now What?". This milestone marks just the beginning of their journey to revolutionize the industry.

## What is Gurwi?

Gurwi is a visual and interactive learning application designed to make learning about anything easy and fun. Whether you're exploring complex topics or mastering new skills, Gurwi transforms the experience into something engaging and accessible.

Recently, Gurwi won the Shipathon and is now available for download on both the **Play Store** and **App Store**.
